About Us

MonAram is a social impact initiative of SpeakGuru Foundation.

SpeakGuru Foundation, bearing registration no. HOME/SRC-5945 dated 17-09-2010, is a foundation that has been providing financial support to children living with cancer, providing educational resources to needy children and, promoting the conservation of our flora and fauna in Nagaland.

MonAram is one of the winners of the First Hackathon edition organised by StartUp Nagaland in partnership with the Department of Industries and Commerce, and Educentre School of Business, 2021.

MonAram will provide a scheme called MonAram Personal Accident package to cover personal accidents through voluntary risk pooling, in which the members can also participate in the management. The package will provide top-class medical treatment at any of our health care providers in Nagaland.
